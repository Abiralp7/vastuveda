Privacy Policy

calendar_today

Effective Date: October 24, 2023

At VastuVeda Designs, we believe that trust is the foundation of any great design. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to protecting the privacy and personal information of our clients, partners, and website visitors. We are dedicated to transparency in how we collect, use, and safeguard your data as we work together to bring architectural visions to life.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services to you:

  • Personal Information: When you inquire about a project, sign up for our newsletter, or contact us, we may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and property location.
  • Project Data: For client projects, we collect specific architectural requirements, site surveys, blueprints, and budget constraints necessary for design delivery.
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect information on how the Service is accessed and used. This Usage Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address), browser type, browser version, and the pages of our Service that you visit.

3. Storage & Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Note on Project Files: Architectural blueprints and sensitive client site data are stored on encrypted servers with restricted access limited to the project team.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your Personally Identifiable Information unless we provide users with advance notice. This does not include website hosting partners and other parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving our users, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release information when it's release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property or safety.
